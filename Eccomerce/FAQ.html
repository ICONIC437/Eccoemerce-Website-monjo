Shipping

What is your shipping policy?

We offer free standard shipping for orders over $50. For orders under $50, shipping rates vary based on the destination and the size and weight of the package. We also offer expedited shipping options for an additional fee.

How long does it take to receive my order?

Orders typically take 3-5 business days to arrive after they have been shipped. Expedited shipping options are available for faster delivery.

Returns

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for a full refund. Items must be in their original condition and packaging. Some items may be subject to a restocking fee.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track the status of your shipment on our website or on the carrier's website.

International Shipping

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to many countries. Shipping rates and delivery times vary based on the destination.
